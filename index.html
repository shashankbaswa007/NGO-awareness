WHO WE ARE

From Chhattisgarh to 28 States — A Story of Change

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla — not with a massive budget or corporate backing, but with an unshakeable belief that ordinary people can drive extraordinary change.

Today, we are a Section 8 registered non-profit headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, operating across India through 6 key initiatives: education, food & clothing distribution, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development.

Our network of 200+ volunteers spans 28 states. We hold certifications including 80G & 12A, CSR-1, NITI Aayog, and ISO 9001:2015 — reflecting our commitment to transparency and quality in every initiative.

Our Mission

Create lasting social impact through education, empowerment, environment, and compassion across every corner of India.

Our Vision

A compassionate, inclusive India where no child is uneducated, no woman is left behind, and no living being suffers in silence.

"

When we started InAmigos in 2020, we had one conviction — that change doesn't wait for the powerful. It begins with the willing. Every volunteer who joins us becomes proof of that conviction.

— Mr. Govind Shukla

OUR JOURNEY

Five Years of Impact — And Counting

From a small initiative in Chhattisgarh to a national movement across 28 states.

2020
Founded Sept 23. Section 8 registered. Project SEVA and BACHPANSHALA launched across Chhattisgarh.
2021
Expanded to 10+ states. Project JEEV launched — stray animal rescue and daily feeding programs begin.
2022
NITI Aayog registration achieved. Project UDAAN launched — rural women empowerment through SHG collaboration.
2023
IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification. Project PRAKRITI — 10,000+ saplings planted across India.
2024
30,000+ interns trained via Project VIKAS. Network grows to 28 states with 200+ volunteers.
2025
50,000+ beneficiaries impacted. CSR-1 registration — now eligible for corporate partnerships nationwide.

THE PURPOSE BEHIND IT ALL

Why We Do What We Do

The InAmigos Foundation is more than an NGO — it's a movement dedicated to fostering a compassionate, inclusive, and sustainable society. Every project addresses a fundamental human need that, if left unmet, holds back entire communities from reaching their full potential.

01

Dignity & Education

Tackling hunger and illiteracy at the root. Project SEVA ensures no one goes to bed hungry, while BachpanShala breaks the cycle of poverty through education — because a child denied learning is a future denied to all.

02

Empowerment & Compassion

Project UDAAN empowers women through self-help groups and financial independence. Project JEEV extends compassion to every living being — feeding 50+ stray animals daily through our volunteer network.

03

Sustainability & Growth

Project PRAKRITI has planted 20,000+ saplings for a greener India. Project VIKAS has trained 30,000+ interns — turning India's youth potential into real-world professions and careers.
